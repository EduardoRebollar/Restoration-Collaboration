About Us

Our mission is to provide support to areas of emergency. We provide training and educational workshops on emergency preparedness and prevention. Just as disasters don’t discriminate in terms of whose lives they destroy; we doesn’t discriminate in whose lives we help rebuild. This is a safe and secure place for everyone to reach out to.



Services

Restoration Collaboration offers many services including Community Training, Workshops, Charity, and Sheltering!


Community Training

Provide community training on how to be prepared for any emergencies or natural disasters

Workshops

Hands-on workshops for what to do when disaster strikes and how you can look for signs to prevent any emergencies

Charity

Donate money and food to those affected by natural disasters

Sheltering

Provide shelter and resources for people in areas of disaster.
